About my name and origin

I've set up this info page because my name seems a bit hard to pronounce to non-Romanians. I've also added a bonus section on Romania :)

Name

The spelling of my name is quite easy, as it doesn't contain any non-standard latin characters. Marius is my given name, and Constantinescu is my family name.

However, the pronunciation of my name is the one which tricks most of the people outside Romania. Here is a pretty decent pronunciation of my name by a robot voice. Taking the first name step by step:

  • Ma is like in "mom" or "march"
  • ri is like in "river" or "wrist". The "r" is trilled, like in most latin languages
  • us is similar to the one in "goose" or "loose", but with a slightly shorter "u"

    • The closest English phonetic transcription is (probably) /mʌrɪʊs kɒnstʌntɪneskʊ/. But in the middle of an English sentence, I sometimes have troubles pronouncing it correctly myself.

Meaning

Marius is a name which appears in the Roman ancient history. It's said to be derived from the Roman god of war Mars. In more recent times, in Romania, it is believed be a male version of the female name Maria (Romanian equivalent for Mary).

According to Wikipedia, it is a common name in Lithuania, Norway and Romania, and other variations appear in Greek (Marios), Italian (Mario), Poland (Mariusz) and Portuguese (Mário).

Romania

I come from Romania. Some basic facts:

  • Romania is in the EU, but doesn't use Euro.
  • We're latins, but with some serious slavic influences. This can be easily noticed in our language: I can understand Italian or Spanish pretty well, but they wouldn't understand Romanian .
  • Romania is the 13th biggest country of Europe and the 10th most populated.
  • Romania has been an independent state since 1877 (and internationally recognized since 1878)
  • The capital and largest city of Romania is Bucharest (approx. 2M people).
