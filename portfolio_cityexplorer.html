CityExplorer

iOS personalized travel app

CityExplore aims to be a personalised travel guide for the users, giving touristic recommendations based on the users' preferences. In its initial phase, the app will only work for the Copenhagen Area, with future versions bringing more destinations.

The app shows the recommended points of interest colour-coded on a map, depending on their "compatibility" score with the user. They can also be viewd as a simple list. Each recommended place comes with details such as address, phone number, opening hours and a short description from Wikipedia.

The frontend is an iOS app, while the backend is written using the Parse.com library, JavaScript and Python.

The app was created in a team of two people, together with Ruxandra Nistor.

Technologies used: iOS SDK, Core Location Framework, ARC, Xcode, Parse.com, MapKit, Google Maps SDK for iOS
