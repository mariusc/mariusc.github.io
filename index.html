Marius Constantinescu

iOS Developer

I'm Marius Constantinescu and I'm an iOS developer

Since March 2014, I've been working at Nodes in Copenhagen.

I'm very passionate about what I do and I always try to keep up to date with the latest news and trends in iOS development.

I help maintain and contribute to many iOS open source projects, most of them on the Nodes iOS GitHub account. I'm one of the main contributors to iOS Goodies, I maintain the most popular open source list of mobile conferences out there, I write stuff either on my blog or on the Nodes engineering blog which sometimes gets picked by some pretty big iOS development newsletters.

I've also given some talks internally at Nodes or at the local iOS developer group.

Besides open source stuff, I also make apps. You can read more about the apps I built and about my competences in my portfolio.

If you want to know more about me, check out the previous long version of my bio, or send me a tweet and ask.
